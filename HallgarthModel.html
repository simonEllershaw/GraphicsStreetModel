Controls

  • Arrow keys: Move camera
  • P: Toggle between 3rd and 1st person perspective
  • WASD: Rotate camera (1st person perspective only)
  • Z and X: Zoom in and out (3rd person perspective only)
  • R: Reset camera
  • B: Toggle bins open and closed
  • G: Toggle garage doors open and closed
  • O: Toggle front doors open and closed
  • L: Toggle street lights on and off
  • T: Move time of day forward
